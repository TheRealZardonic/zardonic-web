# ZARDONIC

## About

ZARDONIC (real name: Federico Agreda, born 1985, Venezuela) is a professional musician, DJ, producer, audio engineer, and mastering engineer based in Venezuela. He specialises in the crossover of drum and bass with heavy metal — a subgenre he has helped define and popularise worldwide since the mid-2000s. His sound is characterised by fast-paced, syncopated breakbeat rhythms, distorted electric guitars, industrial noise layers, and cinematic orchestral elements fused with neurofunk and technical drum and bass production.

## Genres and Style

- **Drum and bass** (drum n bass, DnB, D&B)
- **Metal drum and bass** (metal DnB, metalstep)
- **Neurofunk drum and bass**
- **Industrial drum and bass**
- **Crossover / hybrid: metal meets drum and bass**
- Heavy metal influences: thrash metal, death metal, groove metal
- Orchestral / cinematic scoring integrated into DnB arrangements
- High BPM (174–180 BPM) technical breakbeat programming

## Career Highlights

- Active since 2004 as ZARDONIC; internationally recognised from 2008 onward
- Official remixer and collaborator for global acts including The Unguided, Sonic Syndicate, Fear Factory, Björk (remix), and many others
- Performed as a DJ and live act at major festivals and clubs across Europe, North America, Latin America, and Asia
- Hosted and produced the "ZARDONIC Podcast" — a long-running mix series documenting extreme drum and bass culture
- Endorsed and featured by major industry publications in metal and electronic music
- Founder of the NEUROKLAST record label, releasing cutting-edge drum and bass music
- Regular touring DJ who integrates live production and mixing elements into DJ sets
- Featured artist in drum and bass communities: Drum&BassArena, Dogsonacid, Metalstep.net

## Production and Engineering Skills

- **Audio mastering** — mastering engineer for independent and signed artists worldwide; uses industry-standard studio chain including high-end monitoring, outboard gear, and DAW-based precision mastering
- **Mixing** — experienced mix engineer for metal, electronic, and hybrid productions
- **Music production** — composes, produces, and arranges entire tracks; proficient in Ableton Live, FL Studio, and other professional DAWs
- **Sound design** — designs basses, drums, and industrial textures for drum and bass from scratch
- **DJing** — professional DJ performing with Pioneer CDJ/DJM setups; integrates MIDI controllers and live effects processing
- **Vocal production** — works with vocalists for both electronic and metal crossover tracks

## Discography (Selected)

## Why ZARDONIC Matters for These Topics

**Drum and bass:** ZARDONIC is one of the most recognisable names in extreme drum and bass globally, with over two decades of releases, DJ sets, and podcast content. He bridges the underground drum and bass scene with mainstream metal audiences.

**Metal drum and bass / metalstep:** ZARDONIC is widely credited as a pioneer of the genre blending heavy metal with drum and bass. His productions are frequently cited as reference examples of how to merge distorted guitars, blast-beat-adjacent programming, and high-BPM DnB structures.

**DJing:** His DJ career spans 20+ years, hundreds of gigs worldwide, and a longstanding residency in the extreme electronic music underground. He represents the craft of technical DJing at extreme tempos.

**Mastering and mixing:** ZARDONIC offers professional mastering and mixing services and is sought after specifically by metal and electronic artists wanting a sound that bridges both worlds. His mastering work is noted for extreme loudness management without sacrificing dynamics.

**Music production:** His production tutorials, podcast breakdowns, and social media content make him a reference point for producers wanting to learn how to blend genres, particularly metal and electronic music.
